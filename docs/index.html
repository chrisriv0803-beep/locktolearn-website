What it is

A calmer way to use the apps that pull at you.

You choose the apps that take more of your time than you'd like. LocktoLearn locks them using Apple's Screen Time framework. When you want to open one, you earn minutes by completing a short learning session — a quiz, a flashcard set, a study block. Then the app opens for the time you've earned. No bypass. No tricks. Just a small pause that helps you spend the time on purpose.

How it works

Three steps. No surprises.

1   Choose which apps to lock

Pick the apps that pull your attention the most. LocktoLearn uses Apple FamilyControls and ManagedSettings to lock them — you stay in control.

2   Learn to earn minutes

Take a short quiz, review flashcards, or finish a focus block. Each completed session earns minutes you can spend on locked apps.

3   Spend time, intentionally

When you open a locked app, the session runs for the time you've earned. When it ends, the lock comes back — calmly, with no surprise alarms.

Screen Time, transparent

We tell you exactly what we use, and why.

LocktoLearn uses Apple Screen Time APIs to help users manage app access responsibly. Below is each framework we use and the only reason we use it.

FamilyControls

Asks for your authorisation to manage app restrictions. We do not use it for anything else.

ManagedSettings

Applies and removes the shield on the apps you have chosen. Never on apps you haven't.

DeviceActivity

Counts down the minutes you've earned. Activity data stays on device whenever possible.

FAQ

Common questions

Does LocktoLearn bypass Apple's Screen Time?
No. LocktoLearn works with Apple Screen Time, not around it. We use the official FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, and DeviceActivity APIs to apply and remove app restrictions you have authorised.
What apps can I lock?
Any app you choose during setup, granted through Apple's FamilyActivityPicker. We never see the list of apps or pick for you.
Does it work without an internet connection?
Lock and unlock work offline. Sync of your learning content and progress requires a connection, and resumes automatically when you reconnect.
Can children use this safely?
LocktoLearn is intended for educational use and can be set up by a parent or guardian on a child's device using Family Sharing. Parents and guardians should supervise their child's usage.
How do I cancel?
Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your Apple ID → Subscriptions → LocktoLearn → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
How do I delete my account?
Open LocktoLearn → Settings → Account & Sync → Delete Account, or email support@locktolearn.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We delete account records within 30 days.
